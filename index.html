Saudade Cafe and Antique Shop.

A unique coffee house environment unlike any other in Arrecife. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than welcome to do that), we are a place where you can sit down, study, work, all in a warm environment. We have wifi, calm music and comfortable tables.
If you want to have a lovely time with friends, we have special area that you can book and save specially for your group. We offer a delicious variety of coffee by Borbone Cafe made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages.

All of our dishes are made in-house with all natural ingredients: no chemicals or preservatives, also from local farms, ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health.

You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker and why not, have a look to our unique antique items that are also in sale. We look forward to serving you at Saudade, Monday to Saturday, always happy to see you.
